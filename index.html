Types of Wetpour Repair Kits

Wetpour repair kits come in various types to cater to different repair needs. The main categories include single component repair kits, two component repair kits, and cold cure repair kits, each offering specific benefits and applications.

Single component repair kits are convenient due to their ready-to-use nature, ideal for quick fixes on small surface areas.

They are typically applied in situations where immediate repairs are required to address minor damages like cracks or thinning surfaces.

On the other hand, two component repair kits involve mixing two separate compounds to create a durable solution.

This type is preferred for more extensive damage as it offers enhanced strength and longevity, making it suitable for high traffic areas or regions prone to wear and tear.

Cold cure repair kits are designed for colder climates or situations where rapid curing is essential.

These kits utilise advanced formulas that can set at low temperatures, ensuring efficient repairs even in challenging weather conditions.
